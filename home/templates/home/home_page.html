Frequently Asked Questions

Because ‘Trust Us’ Isn’t a Strategy.

What is odds trading?

Odds trading is a mathematical way to bet, focusing on value bets with small odds for consistent gains. It’s about strategy, not luck.

How do I start with Databet?

Download the app, fund your Betway account, then follow our daily ticket or manual tips—staking just 1–5 % of your bankroll each time.

Which sports do you cover?

We focus on major football (soccer) leagues worldwide, including the Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, and more.

How do you calculate your tips?

Through proprietary algorithms that analyze historical odds movements, team form, and statistical indicators. Each tip is scored for value.

Do I need football expertise to use Databet?

No, our app is for everyone—daily tickets let you copy and trade, while previews offer insights for those who want to learn.

Can I contact you with suggestions?

Absolutely—reach out via our contact form, call or social channels. We thrive on feedback and community input!
